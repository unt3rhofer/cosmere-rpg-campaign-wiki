## Stormweaver
- Shardblade owned by [Terelin Atanam](../NPCs/terelin-atanam.md), inherited from his father [Osilin Atanam](../NPCs/other.md#osilin-atanam)
- Found by Terelin on an expedition to the [Purelake](../locations/jah-keved.md#purelake)
- Wide long slightly curved blade with a stormclouds motif on its sides ending in a curved tip like the front of a storm
